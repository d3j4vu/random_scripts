Event Details

Holi is all about celebrating the colors and vitality of spring, with family and friends – and we figure about 5500 lbs of food-grade colors should be enough to get you started with the celebrations!

Your first time at Asha Holi? Make sure you read our site on how Asha Holi works! Come join us in this day long party, and help us raise funds for education projects for disadvantaged children!

Check out our website for more information about Asha for Education at Stanford. Read about our featured project. 

NOTE: NON-STUDENT TICKETING WILL START SHORTLY

We had a sell-out event last year and again have *ONLINE ONLY* ticket sales. Tickets will not be sold at the venue. Tell your friends!

What are you waiting for? Pick your day i.e. April 5th or 6th! Awesomeness guaranteed on both days! 

Important: 

  • Student/Stanford Student ticket purchasers will have to show a valid student ID on the day.
  • In the event you are unable to produce a valid student ID, the ticket will be considered a “General Ticket” and you will be required to make up the price difference prior to entering the field.
  • Children 5 years old or younger do not need tickets!
